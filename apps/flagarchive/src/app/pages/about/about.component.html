A Historical Flag Database

Flag Archive is a comprehensive online database dedicated to the visual history of flags from around the world. The mission is to preserve, document, and share the stories behind national, regional, and historical flags, making them accessible to anyone interested in vexillology, design, or global history.

What is Flag Archive?

Flag Archive is a digital archive where users can explore flags by country, time period, region, or political status. Whether you're searching for a defunct empire's banner, a colonial-era variant, or a current national flag, this platform offers flag images and detailed background information for each entry.

Why Flag History Matters

Flags are more than just designs — they are symbols of identity, sovereignty, resistance, and change. Studying historical flags helps us understand the evolution of nations, political movements, and cultural shifts. At Flag Archive, the aim is to make this data available to all enthusiasts worldwide.

Key Features

Searchable Flag Database

Discover flags by country, continent, time period (e.g. 18th-century flags), type (e.g. maritime, military, colonial), or political status (current, historical, proposed).

Detailed Metadata

Each flag entry includes adoption dates, historical context, design notes, usage, and flag variations.

Cross-Referencing Tools

Explore visual connections between flags across different countries and eras, from design lineage to geopolitical influence.

Who Should Use Flag Archive?

Historians and Researchers

For exploring national symbols, independence movements, or colonial history.

Educators and Students

Teaching or learning about world history, geography, or visual culture.

Designers and Creatives

Needing authentic references for period-accurate design work.

Vexillologists and Hobbyists

Passionate about the study of flags and their meanings.

How The Archive is Build

  • Usage of historical sources, including national archives, flag atlases, government publications, and scholarly research.
  • The metadata tagging system allows for precise categorization, making it easy to find what you're looking for.
  • User contributions — from image submissions to historical corrections, the community plays an essential role in keeping the archive accurate and up to date.

From ancient empires to newly established nations, from independence movements to political unions, Flag Archive captures the changing landscape of world history — one flag at a time.
